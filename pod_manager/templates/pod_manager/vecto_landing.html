Vecto

The intelligent, multi-tenant podcast distribution engine built for creators.

Vecto is a Patreon-integrated feed mixer. It seamlessly blends premium and public feeds on a listener-by-listener basis, validating subscriptions in real-time. When a subscription lapses, Vecto handles the feed downgrade automatically—no more juggling RSS links or asking listeners to resubscribe in their podcast apps.

Dynamic Blending

Subscribers get a single, personalized RSS feed containing all the premium shows they have access to, effortlessly mixed together.

Auto-Downgrading

If a pledge fails or lapses, Vecto instantly downgrades that user's feed to the public, ad-supported versions without breaking their app.

Multi-Tenant Routing

Host dozens of independent podcast networks on a single Vecto instance, each with their own white-labeled custom domain and Patreon logic.
